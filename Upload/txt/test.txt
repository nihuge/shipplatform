Vt ＝ Vb × [1+3α(t - 20)]

t ----- 舱壁平均温度,℃；
α----- 舱材料的线膨胀系数；
α=.000012/℃。

Vt ＝ Vb × [1+3α(t - 20)]
Vt -- Capacity after corrected of the temperature,m3.
Vb -- the capacity shown in the tank capacity table,m3.
t ---- the average temperature of bulkhead, ℃.

2. 空高: 液面至计量管上计量基准点的高度；
实高: 液面至计量管下计量基准点的高度；
纵倾值: 即船艉吃水值与船艏吃水值之差；
基准高度: 计量管上、下计量基准点间的
垂直高度。
3. 在容量表中， 当实高为零时的容量数据，
表示舱底量的最大值， 若舱内无液体时，
则实际货物量应为零。
4. 实高查表时，实高应加上纵倾、横倾修正
值；空高查表时，空高应减去纵倾、横倾
修正值。
2. Ullage,the distance between the free surface of the liquid and
upper reference point. Sounding,the distance between the
dipping datum point and the free surface of the liquid. Trim,
the difference between the values of draft fore and draft Aft.
Reference height, the distance between the dipping datum
point and the upper reference point.
3. In the tank capacity table, when the sounding is at the level
of zero, the capacity data will express the maximum capacity
at bottom cabin, when there is no liquid in the under-deck,
the amount of real goods is zero.
4. Sounding checkup : Trim & list correction should be added
to the sounding. Ullage checkup : Trim & list correction
should be subtracted from the ullage.
